What Are Meat Lugs?

Meat lugs (also called tote boxes or lug boxes) are shallow, rectangular plastic containers with molded handles used throughout meat processing facilities. They hold meat trimmings, ground product, portioned cuts, and processed meat as it moves through the plant.

Elipacko manufactures plastic meat lugs in food-grade polypropylene — lightweight, easy to sanitize, stackable, and built to perform in the demanding cold, wet conditions of a meat processing facility. Made from recycled PP and fully recyclable at end of life.
